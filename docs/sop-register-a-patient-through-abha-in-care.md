### ObjectiveThis SOP explains how to register a patient through ABHA in Care, including generating a new ABHA number, verifying the patient, and completing the registration. It is intended to help staff complete the process accurately and consistently.

### Key Steps**1. Open the patient registration area** [0:01](https://loom.com/share/cf16417a66904565b26f7fe0758328ae?t=1)

![image-20260508-092514.png](images/image-20260508-092514.png)
- Review all data shown on the final page carefully.

- Verify that the patient information, ABHA details, and consent selections are correct.

- Click **Submit** to complete the registration process.

### Cautionary Notes
- Ensure the correct **facility** is selected before starting.

- Use the patient’s **accurate Aadhaar number** and verify that consent has been obtained before proceeding.

- Confirm the OTP is entered correctly and within the valid time window.

- Double-check all patient details before submitting, as errors may affect registration accuracy.

- If the system shows an existing number or address, verify whether to link existing details or create a new one based on the patient’s situation.

### Tips for Efficiency
- Keep the patient’s Aadhaar-linked mobile phone available before starting verification.

- Review required consents and location selections in advance to avoid delays.

- Use the suggested ABHA address format when possible to speed up creation.

- Confirm patient details at each step to reduce the need for corrections later.

- Download the ABHA details immediately after creation for easy recordkeeping.
