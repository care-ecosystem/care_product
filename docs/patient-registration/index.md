## Registration Workflow

1. **Choose Method** - Manual entry or ABHA-based registration
2. **Enter Details** - Patient demographics and identification
3. **Add Tags** - Categorize patient for filtering and reporting
4. **Assign Access** - Link users who need to access the record
5. **Verify** - Review and confirm all information
